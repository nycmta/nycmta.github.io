The Pulse of NYC: Weather, Events, and the Subway

Welcome

New York City is a living organism, and the subway is its circulatory system. But what happens when that system is stressed?

This project explores how Extreme Weather (from heat waves to nor’easters) and Mega-Events (like the Marathon and Pride) alter the rhythm of the city. By analyzing over 2 million hourly ridership records from 2024-2025, we uncover the hidden patterns of the daily commute.
